1  Introduction

You can help Uludag Project with help listed localization groups. Because of our primarily software decisions, if you want make your localization works useful directly for this project you can contact us from our mailing lists1. Furthermore you can contact us for changed or missing localization projects and any any change you want on this document. Also you may want to reach these listed groups to ask them to translate an open source software, a project documentation or even a project web site into Turkish.

Listing of localization groups is categorized and in alphabetical order.

2  Official Localization Groups

2.1  GNOME Turkey



They work on GNOME's localization and promotion. They help developing of software.
Website:
http://www.gnome.org.tr
Status Page:
http://l10n-status.gnome.org/
E-mail List:
http://mail.gnome.org/mailman/listinfo/gnome-turk

2.2  GNU-TR



They prepare Turkish support for GNU softwares that works on Linux.
Website:
http://gnu-tr.sourceforge.net
E-mail List:
http://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/gnu-tr-u12a

2.3  KDE Turkey



They work on KDE's localization and promotion.
Website:
http://www.kde.org.tr
E-mail List:
http://www.kde.org.tr/mailman/listinfo/yerellestirme

2.4  Mozilla Turkey

They work on localization of web tools that developed by Mozilla Foundation.
Website:
http://www.mozilla.org.tr
E-mail List:
http://lists.mozilla.org.tr/mailman/listinfo/mozilla

2.5  Turkish OpenOffice.org

They work on OpenOffice.org's localization and promotion. They help developing of software to make it 100% Turkish compatible.
Website:
http://www.openoffice.org.tr
Status Page:
http://ootrans.iqchoice.com/
E-mail List:
http://tr.openoffice.org/servlets/ProjectMailingListList

2.6  PostgreSQL Turkish



They prepare Turkish documents and Turkish support for PostgreSQL database server and additional tools.
Website:
http://sourceforge.net/projects/pgsqlturkce
Status Page:
http://developer.postgresql.org/~petere/nlsstatus/#tcurrent
